Sometimes you just need to type. Not collaborate in real time, not build a complex template, not manage citations — just open something and write. For that task, a clean, fast typing tool beats a feature-heavy application every time.

**[Online WordPad](/)** is built for exactly this. It loads in seconds, puts the cursor in a clean page, and stays out of your way while you work.

## Why WordPad Is Great for Typing

Most full-featured word processors are optimized for people who use them all day, every day. That means menus, toolbars, sidebars, and panels that are useful for power users but visually overwhelming when you just need to draft something quickly.

Online WordPad takes a different approach:

- **Instant load**: no splash screen, no loading progress bar, no onboarding wizard
- **Clean interface**: a single toolbar at the top, a clean white page below
- **No distractions**: no chat panel, no version history sidebar, no comment threads cluttering your view
- **No account prompt**: nothing interrupts you asking you to log in or upgrade
- **Keyboard-first**: most things you need while typing are one shortcut away

The result is a tool that gets out of your way and lets you write.

## Features That Help With Typing

Beyond the clean interface, Online WordPad includes several features that directly support a good typing experience:

- **Slash command menu**: type `/` anywhere in your document to bring up an insert menu. Add a table, image, heading, or page break without lifting your hands from the keyboard.
- **Auto-save**: your document saves automatically to your browser as you type. No Ctrl+S habit required.
- **Unlimited undo and redo**: make a mistake or change your mind. Undo as many steps as you need.
- **Find and replace**: locate any word or phrase in a long document without scrolling.
- **Emoji picker**: insert emoji inline without switching to a separate keyboard or app.

## Formatting While You Type

You do not need to stop typing to format text. Online WordPad supports the standard keyboard shortcuts that work in every major word processor:

| Shortcut | Action |
|---|---|
| **Ctrl + B** | Bold |
| **Ctrl + I** | Italic |
| **Ctrl + U** | Underline |
| **Ctrl + Z** | Undo |
| **Ctrl + Y** | Redo |
| **Ctrl + A** | Select all |
| **Ctrl + C** | Copy |
| **Ctrl + X** | Cut |
| **Ctrl + V** | Paste |
| **Ctrl + P** | Print |

On Mac, replace Ctrl with Cmd. These shortcuts work exactly as expected — there are no conflicts or overrides from the editor.

## Auto-Save So You Never Lose Your Work

Losing a document because you forgot to save is a frustration that Online WordPad eliminates entirely. Your content is saved to **browser local storage** automatically as you type. This means:

- Closing the tab accidentally does not erase your work
- Your browser crashing does not erase your work
- Losing your internet connection does not affect saving

When you return to [the editor](/pad) in the same browser, your document picks up exactly where you left it.

For a permanent copy that survives browser data clearing or device changes, use the **Export** button to download your document as a .docx, HTML, or plain text file.

## Tips for Distraction-Free Typing

Getting into a focused writing flow is easier with the right setup:

1. **Use full-screen mode**: press F11 in most browsers to hide the browser chrome and maximize your writing space
2. **Type `/` instead of reaching for the mouse**: the slash command menu keeps your hands on the keyboard
3. **Ignore formatting until you are done**: write first, format after. The toolbar will be there when you need it
4. **Set a word count goal**: use the slash command to check your progress, or paste into a word counter when you're done
5. **Close other tabs**: Online WordPad cannot close your other distractions for you, but one less barrier helps
