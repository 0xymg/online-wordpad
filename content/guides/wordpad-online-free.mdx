## Where Your Data Goes

Your document never leaves your device. **Online WordPad saves exclusively to your browser's localStorage** — a local storage area that only your browser can access on your machine. Nothing is transmitted to any server.

This means:

- No account holds your data
- No cloud sync happens without your involvement
- Your document persists across page refreshes and browser restarts
- Clearing your browser cache or localStorage will erase the saved document

If you want a permanent backup, use **File → Export** to download a .docx or .txt file to your device. That file is yours entirely.
